About Our GitHub

Welcome to the official guide for the Maryland State Innovation Team's GitHub presence. Here, we share our open-source projects, datasets, analytical tools, and methodologies. Our goal is to foster transparency, collaboration, and innovation in public service.

This page will help you navigate our repositories and understand the resources available. Whether you're a developer, researcher, policymaker, or curious citizen, we hope you find something valuable.

Our Mission on GitHub

  • To openly share code and data for public benefit.
  • To encourage community contributions and feedback.
  • To showcase innovative data-driven solutions for Maryland.
  • To provide resources that support evidence-based policymaking.

Our Methodologies

We are committed to rigorous and transparent analytical methods. Our approach often includes:

  • Agile project management for iterative development.
  • Human-centered design principles in tool development.
  • Statistical modeling and machine learning for predictive insights.
  • Version control (Git) for all code and analytical scripts.
  • Open standards and formats where possible.

Detailed documentation for specific project methodologies can be found within their respective GitHub repositories.
